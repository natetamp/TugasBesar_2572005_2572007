THIS GENERATION'S BIGGEST ACT

Gen 1 · Bada Kiha / Independent · Debuted 1992

Seo Taiji & Boys

서태지와 아이들 — Seo Taeji wa Aideul

The legendary trio that revolutionized the South Korean music industry by blending American hip-hop, breakdancing, and rock with Korean lyrics. They are widely credited with birthing the modern K-pop industry, breaking broadcasting censorship, and giving a voice to the youth of the 1990s.

Gen 2 · SM Entertainment · Debuted 2007

Girls' Generation

소녀시대 — Sonyeo Sidae (SNSD)

Commonly referred to as "The Nation's Girl Group," this iconic group solidified the global presence of the Hallyu wave. Renowned for their flawless synchronization, infectious hooks, and immense cultural impact, they became the first girl group to win the prestigious Golden Disc Awards Daesang for Album of the Year.

Gen 3 · Big Hit Entertainment · Debuted 2013

BTS

방탄소년단 — Bangtan Sonyeondan

Seven members from across South Korea who went from a small indie label to selling out stadiums on every continent. The first K-pop group to top the Billboard Hot 100, nominated for Grammys, and credited with generating over $5 billion for the Korean economy.

Gen 4 · JYP Entertainment · Debuted 2019

ITZY

있지 — It Ji

A powerhouse girl group that burst onto the scene with a refreshing, high-energy concept centered around self-love and confidence. Known for their complex, razor-sharp choreography and explosive performances, they achieved a historic "Rookie Grand Slam" by winning rookie awards across all major Korean music ceremonies.

Gen 5 · WAKEONE Entertainment · Debuted 2023

ZEROBASEONE

제로베이스원 — Jero Beiseu Won

A global boy group formed through the survival reality show 'Boys Planet'. They made history by becoming the very first K-pop group to sell over 1 million copies of their debut album on its release day, instantly establishing themselves as leaders of the fifth generation.
