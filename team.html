BEHIND THE PROJECT

MEET THE TEAM

Two Informatics Engineering students from Universitas Kristen Maranatha who built KJOURNAL from scratch — a passion project dedicated to documenting the full story of K-Pop culture.

KJ 26 UKM

"We didn't just build a website. We documented a cultural revolution, generation by generation."

KJOURNAL was created as a final project for the Web Dasar course at Universitas Kristen Maranatha. What started as an assignment became something we genuinely cared about — a place where K-Pop history is told properly, with context and depth.
